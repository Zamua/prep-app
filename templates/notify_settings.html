Notifications

When prep nudges.

Push notifications arrive even when prep isn't open — on iOS this only works if you've installed the app to your home screen. Choose how often you'd like to be nudged; you can change this any time.

How notifications work
  • Daily digest — one notification per day at the hour you choose, summarizing what's due across all decks. Best if you want a single morning prompt.
  • When ready — fires the first moment your due-pile crosses a threshold (default 3 cards), and won't re-fire for 4 hours after, regardless of new arrivals. Best if your study windows are flexible.
  • Off — no pushes. The app still works, you just open it on your own schedule.

All modes respect a quiet-hours window so you don't get pinged overnight. Time-zone is auto-detected from this device.
