What you get

  • AI-built

    Type a topic — paste a chapter, a paper, a syllabus. An AI agent plans the deck for you to review and accept. Or write the cards yourself.

  • FSRS

    Each card schedules itself based on how well you recall it. The hard ones return right before you'd forget; the easy ones get out of your way.

  • PWA

    Installs on your phone like a native app. Push notifies when a stack comes due. Study for 90 seconds on the train, then close it.

  • BYOK

    Generation, grading, and edits run against an API key you supply. Your model, your bill — no shared pool to throttle against.
