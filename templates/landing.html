prep

A standing library
of questions.

Spaced-repetition flashcards for the things you want to keep — a topic in, a deck out, then a forgetting curve that re-surfaces each card right before you'd lose it.

How it works

Describe it, study it.

Type a topic — capitals of every country, the Krebs cycle, Postgres MVCC — and an AI agent plans a deck for you to review and accept. Or write the cards yourself. Either way, what comes out is yours.

The schedule

Tuned to you.

Each card schedules itself based on how well you remember it — FSRS picks the next review at the moment your recall is starting to fade. The ones you know slip into the background; the ones you don't quietly come back around.

Bring your own key

Your model, your bill.

Generation, grading, and transforms run against an API key you supply. No usage caps imposed by us, no shared pool to throttle against, and your prompts and answers never leave the path between you and the model you chose.

On every device

Installs as an app.

Open it in a browser, add to home screen, and prep behaves like a native app — offline-tolerant, push-notified when a stack of cards comes due, sized for one-thumb study on the train.
