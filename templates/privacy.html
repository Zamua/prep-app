Privacy

What we keep, and what we don't.

Plain English. No tracking pixels, no data brokers, no surprises. If anything here is unclear or sounds wrong, email hello@prepcards.app and we'll fix the page.

What we collect

Only what's necessary to run the product:

  • Account identity — your email and a Clerk-issued user id. We use Clerk for sign-in; their privacy policy covers what they store on their end. We get back a stable id and your email, no more.
  • Your decks and cards — every question, answer, topic prompt, and review history you create. Stored in a SQLite database on the prep server.
  • Push subscription endpoints — when you enable notifications, your device's push endpoint URL + keys. Used only to deliver the notifications you ask for.
  • Your AI provider key, if you supply one — encrypted at rest. See the next section.

Your AI key, if you bring one

On your agent settings page you can paste an Anthropic API key. The key is encrypted with AES-256-GCM before it's written to the database; the master key needed to decrypt it lives in a separate permission-restricted file on the server, outside the database volume.

When you trigger an AI feature, the encrypted blob is decrypted just before the call to Anthropic. The plaintext key only exists in memory for the duration of that single request — it's never logged, never sent anywhere except api.anthropic.com, and never returned to your browser after you save it. Your AI prompts and the model's responses go straight between the prep server and Anthropic; we don't proxy them through any third party.

You can rotate the key any time by pasting a new one (the old row is replaced), or delete it with the Remove key button.

What we don't collect

  • No analytics — no Google Analytics, no Plausible, no Mixpanel, no first-party tracking pixels. The server logs HTTP request paths and timings for debugging; it doesn't tie those to your account or build a profile.
  • No advertising — we don't run ads and we don't sell anything to ad networks.
  • No payment data — there's nothing to charge for. If that changes we'd add a notice here and never store card numbers on our servers.

Who else sees your data

  • Clerk handles sign-in. Your email + a hashed password (or your Google OAuth identity if you sign in that way) live in their system. They don't see your decks or AI keys.
  • Anthropic sees the prompts and responses for your AI calls — that's the whole point of bringing your key. Their data-retention terms apply to those calls under your account, not ours.
  • Nobody else. We don't share or sell your data to anyone.

Where the servers live

Prep runs on a single Hetzner-hosted Linux server in the United States. The database is local sqlite. Backups are pulled by the same operator who runs the box, encrypted at rest.

Deleting your data

Delete your account through Clerk's account portal. When you do, Clerk sends prep a user.deleted webhook; prep cascades that into a delete across every table that references your user id, including your decks, cards, reviews, push subscriptions, and any stored AI key. The database is purged of your rows during that cascade.
